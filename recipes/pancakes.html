The Fluffiest Pancakes Ever!

Description

These pancakes are super soft and fluffy; each mouthful melting in your mouth! The key is to have the perfect batter - thick but able to run slowly off the spoon.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar or sweetener
  • 4 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1 large egg

Steps

  1. Combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda and salt in a large bowl. Make a well in the center and add milk, slightly cooled melted butter, vanilla and egg.
  2. Use a wire whisk to whisk the wet ingredients first before slowly folding them into the dry ingredients. Mix together until smooth.
  3. Set batter aside and allow to rest while heating up the pan or griddle.
  4. Heat a nonstick pan or griddle over low-medium heat and wipe over with a little butter to lightly grease the pan. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the pan and spread out gently into a round shape with the back of ladle or measuring cup.
  5. When the underside is golden and bubbles begin to appear on the surface, flip with a spatula and cook until golden. Repeat with remaining batter.
  6. Serve with honey, maple syrup, fruit, yogurt, or enjoy plain!
